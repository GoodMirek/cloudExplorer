How to migrate data between S3 accounts:

1. Load the destination account and create or select a bucket to migrate to.
2. From the menu "Snapshots and Migration", click "Set as Migration/Snapshot Account and Bucket".
3. Switch to the origin account and select the bucket to migrate.
4. Under the "Snapshots and Migration", select "Migrate bucket to another S3 account".
5. Click Start Migration
