Guide to the Toolbar Buttons on the Explorer tab.

The numbering goes from left to right.

1. Download selected file.
2. Switch to Upload tab.
3. Delete selected file(s).
4. Properties of selected file.
5. Edit selected file.
6. Select/Deselect all.
7. Play selected music file(s).
8. View versions of selected file.
9. View selected image.
